Request DOI/ARK

A digital object identifier (DOI) and a archival resource key (ARK) is like an address for your electronic file, allowing people to find your data on the web. Forever. The best part is that a DOI allows you and others to cite your data just like a publication, giving you and your data-coauthors credit for your hard work. For more information on DOI/ARK and how to use them see University of Idaho Library Page.
