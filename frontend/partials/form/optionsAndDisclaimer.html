Request DOI/ARK

A digital object identifier (DOI) and an archival resource key (ARK) are like addresses for your electronic file, allowing people to find your data on the web. Forever. The best part is that a DOI allows you and others to cite your data just like a publication, giving you and your data-coauthors credit for your hard work. For more information on DOI/ARK and how to use them see the University of Idaho Library Page.
